Coventry Crime Hotspot Analysis

MSc Dissertation Project – 2025

Interactive Maps

This site hosts interactive Folium maps developed as part of an analysis of crime patterns in Coventry (2018–2025), with a focus on the impact of the 2021 UK City of Culture designation.

  • Total Recorded Crimes by LSOA (2018–2025)
    Choropleth map showing cumulative crime counts across the study period.
  • K-Means Clustering of Crime & Deprivation (2019)
    Three clusters: low, moderate, and high deprivation/crime areas.
  • DBSCAN Density Clustering & Outliers (2019)
    Density-based clustering highlighting core hotspots and situational outliers near nightlife venues.
  • Random Forest Predicted High-Risk Hotspots (2019)
    Machine learning predictions for violence and sexual offences risk.

For full methodology, results, and discussion, please refer to the dissertation document.
